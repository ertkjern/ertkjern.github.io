My first blog post! Using Hugo!

Jan 17, 2017 #blog #hugo #casper

I have decided to try this thing known as blogging. Not because I feel the need to share what I do with anyone, but mostly for learning. When I look at a new programming languages, tools, frameworks or anything, I will sometimes write a small blog post, right here, or maybe a tiny tutorial. By doing this I can allways come back to the blog and use it as a reference, or maybe someone else might learn or find any of my blog posts interesting.

As you have probarbly figured out, this blog post will mostly contain tech related topics. And since this blog post is built on a static web page generator known as Hugo, the next post will be about that.

I hope you will enjoy my writings, if not, I will enjoy it myself!

Have a great day!
